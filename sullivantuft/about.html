About Me

Hello! I'm Sullivan Tuft, a senior at Brigham Young University–Idaho pursuing a degree in Software Engineering with an emphasis in Software Management. I’m passionate about building efficient, user-focused software and thrive in collaborative, problem-solving environments.

My technical experience centers around C# and the .NET framework, but I also enjoy working with React, Python, Kotlin, and Dart. I’ve built web and mobile applications using modern tools like Firebase, APIs, and Git for version control.

Alongside my technical work, I bring over a year of hands-on experience in team settings that require strong communication, customer service, and leadership—skills I developed both professionally and while serving a mission in West Virginia.

I'm currently working at American Construction Supply, where I provide on-site service, coordinate logistics, and ensure timely deliveries with professionalism and attention to detail.

When I'm not coding or studying, I'm most likely learning from real-world projects, playing basketball, or working toward graduation in April 2026.
